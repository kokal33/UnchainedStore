Club der Visionäre & Unchained Music
making history

Make history with us

We're super-excited to announce the first ever live streaming by Club der Visionäre, one of the most notorious clubs from the world capital of electronic music- Berlin.

When & where: 1st of May at 10am (GMT+2) here at unchained-music.com/stream

What about the fundraising? The money raised will go to CDV, which burned down two years ago, to the artists involved in this project and towards rebuilding the music community that suffered immensely during these devastating stage for the live music industry. For this last part, we'll join forces with MusiCares, a charity by Grammy.com, which provides a safety net of critical assistance for music people in times of need.

There will be 10 "CDV's exclusive live anniversary set" NFTs sold via this event. They will be auctioned one by one, lasting throughout the whole week (until the 8th of May).

We want to spread the message of musicians being there for other musicians, stepping together to help rebuild and refresh the music community.
